# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Shoppers browsing K-fashion items on mobile and desktop. They need to find products quickly, judge style and fit from photos, choose available sizes, save favorites, and complete checkout without confusion.

## Product Purpose

Bamblue Store is a fashion e-commerce storefront for browsing, wishlisting, and buying K-fashion products. Success means products feel desirable and trustworthy while the shopping, cart, payment, and order-status flows remain clear and reliable.

## Brand Personality

Sweet, premium, playful. The interface should feel soft and fashion-forward without becoming childish, cluttered, or marketplace-generic.

## Anti-references

Do not make the experience feel like Shopee or Lazada. Avoid crowded promo-heavy layouts, noisy badges, childish decoration, excessive purple, and generic marketplace patterns that weaken the boutique identity.

## Design Principles

Prioritize shopping confidence: product photos, price, stock, size, and CTA states should be immediately understandable.

Keep the boutique mood: use sweetness through restraint, spacing, imagery, and careful pink accents rather than visual noise.

Make payment and order status explicit: every checkout step needs clear feedback, safe failure states, and no ambiguous pending states.

Design for mobile first: product discovery, cart review, wishlist, and payment confirmation must work comfortably on narrow screens.

Use motion to clarify state changes: animation should reveal content without ever hiding usable information or blocking interaction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support reduced motion, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, readable Thai and English typography, and non-color-only feedback for stock, payment, and error states.
